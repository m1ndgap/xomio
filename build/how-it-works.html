Our listings translation delivers more leads

How it works:

  • We import your Brand or MLS listings into our Listings Translation Module
  • 20 years of expertise is applied to deliver listings translation in 20 languages
  • Foreign language search engines index your listings and drive consumers to your Xomio site
  • When a foreign language visitor views your Xomio site, listings are displayed in translation
  • Consumers find and view the listings they are interested in, registering and receiving your multi language marketing mails
